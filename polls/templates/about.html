Our Goal

For our MSc project, we were tasked with creating an easy-to-use tool to aid machine learning (ML) practitioners in the accurate and justified selection of a performance metric for their specific task/use case. As ML systems are increasingly being integrated into critical decision-making processes worldwide, this tool is developed with a general audience in mind, allowing users from all domains and backgrounds to access valuable information. We hope that this tool can be used effectively as a reliable starting framework for legitimate metric selection.
